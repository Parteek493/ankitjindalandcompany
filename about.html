About Us

Our Journey & Expertise

Founded in 2017 by Sh. Subhash Jindal, A J & Co. has grown into a trusted name in infrastructure development. We have successfully executed projects for BSF, RSAMB, AIR, PWD Rajasthan, Medical & Health Department, MGSU, Rajuvas, RSRDC, CWC, RMSA, PWD (NH), CPWD, BRO, MES, and more.

Driven by hard work, dedication, and commitment, we are fully equipped to plan and execute a wide range of infrastructure projects, including buildings, roads, sports courts, and specialized works.

Our team of highly skilled professionals ensures uncompromising quality and timely completion of every project.
